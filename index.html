About the Firm

Who is Viraj Infra & Services?

Viraj Infra & Services is a trusted execution partner in the construction and infrastructure sector, specializing in RCC structural works and skilled manpower solutions across Gurgaon, Delhi NCR & Haryana.

We undertake residential and commercial projects, delivering end-to-end structural execution — from foundation to top slab — with our in-house teams of shuttering, reinforcement, and site workforce.

Alongside construction, we provide reliable manpower services tailored for project requirements, ensuring the right workforce, at the right time, with complete site coordination and supervision.

Our strength lies in speed, precision, and consistency, enabling us to handle large-scale projects while maintaining strict quality and safety standards.

We are committed to building long-term partnerships with builders, contractors, and organizations by delivering on-time, high-quality, and dependable execution services.
