About the Firm

Who is Viraj Infra & Services?

Viraj Infra & Services is engaged in providing comprehensive infrastructure and manpower support services. The firm undertakes construction and civil contract works such as building construction, road work, site development, maintenance, and other related infrastructure projects.

In addition to construction activities, the company also provides skilled and unskilled manpower supply for various sectors including construction sites, hotels, guest houses, offices, industrial units, housekeeping services, and other operational requirements.

The firm is committed to delivering reliable services with quality workmanship, timely execution, and efficient workforce management to meet the requirements of private companies, contractors, and government organizations.

How We Work

From your first call to final handover — a clear, stress-free process with no surprises.

01

Free Consultation

Call or WhatsApp us. We discuss your plot size, budget, and requirements — completely free.

02

Our engineer visits your plot, assesses soil and structure, and gives you an honest estimate.

03
